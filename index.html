Who we are

We are the Major Explorer Group, or M.E.G. for short. The M.E.G. was founded in 1886 and has strived to better humanity in the Backrooms. We have several outposts in several Levels as a way to aid wanderers in their journey. We are also responsible for the exploration of Levels that are discovered, and the documentation of the Levels, Objects and Entities. documented info go to the Database
